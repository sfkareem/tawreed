About Tawreed

Intelligent Construction Procurement Assistant.

Material Scheduling & Procurement

Tawreed streamlines procurement by organizing scattered material data from design documents and Bill of Quantities (BoQ) into ready-to-order procurement packages.

It automates the laborious process of filtering and grouping materials so engineers can focus on technical validation and supplier relations.
